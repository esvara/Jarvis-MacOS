# Code Of Conduct

## Our Standard

Be respectful, direct, and constructive.

Expected behavior:

- assume good intent
- give technical feedback without personal attacks
- keep discussions focused on the code, behavior, and tradeoffs
- welcome contributors with different backgrounds and experience levels

Unacceptable behavior:

- harassment or discrimination
- insults, threats, or intimidation
- doxxing or publication of private information
- disruptive trolling or bad-faith review behavior

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or block participation for behavior that harms the project or its contributors.
